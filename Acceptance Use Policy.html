SOP - Acceptance Use Policy

General Explanation

The acceptable use policy is designed to ensure that all hospital staff utilize the network, hardware, and software in a secure and appropriate way. This helps in maintaining patient confidentiality and safeguarding the integrity of the hospital's systems.

All Aspects Addressed for Networks, Hardware, and Software

Networks: Hospital staff are only allowed to use hospital networks for work-related activities. Unauthorized access or use of the network for personal purposes is strictly prohibited.

Hardware: Hospital-issued devices should only be used for work-related tasks. Staff must not install or alter unauthorized equipment.

Software: Only approved software should be installed on hospital computers. Employees are prohibited from downloading or using personal software on hospital-issued devices.

Management of Policy Violations

Disciplinary action will be taken against employees found in violation of the acceptable use policy. This could range from written warnings to termination, depending on the severity of the violation.

For example, if a staff member installs unauthorized software that compromises patient data, they could face immediate termination.
