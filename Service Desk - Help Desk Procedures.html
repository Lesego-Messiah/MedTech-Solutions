SOP – Service Desk/Help Desk Procedures

Roles and Responsibilities Included and Relevant:

  • Level 1 Support: Offers preliminary assistance for common issues, such as password changes and resolving basic software problems. They respond to initial help calls and escalate more complex issues to Level 2 support.
  • Level 2 Support: Handles more complex technical issues, such as device failures or network outages, requiring deeper troubleshooting. They report directly to the IT Manager and ensure urgent issues are resolved promptly.
